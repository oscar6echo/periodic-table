# Educational Series: Quantum Mechanics

This directory contains a deep dive into the physics that builds the periodic table, from the Schrödinger equation to the 118 elements.

## Chapters

1. **[Chapter 1: The Heart of Quantum Mechanics](chap_1.md)**  
   Introduction to the Schrödinger Equation, wave functions, and operators.
2. **[Chapter 2: The Quantum Warm-Up (Particle in a 1D Box)](chap_2.md)**  
   Understanding quantization and boundary conditions in a simple system.
3. **[Chapter 3: The 3D Hydrogen Atom](chap_3.md)**  
   Moving to three dimensions using spherical coordinates and separation of variables.
4. **[Chapter 4: The Angular Solutions (Orbital Shapes)](chap_4.md)**  
   How Legendre polynomials define the characteristic shapes of $s, p, d,$ and $f$ orbitals.
5. **[Chapter 5: The Energy Shells (Radial Solutions)](chap_5.md)**  
   Laguerre polynomials, energy quantization, and the reality of multi-electron atoms.
6. **[Chapter 6: The Final Piece (Spin and the Periodic Table)](chap_6.md)**  
   Spin, the Pauli exclusion principle, Aufbau principle, and how the periodic table emerges.

## Tools & Annex

- **[Mathematical Annex](annex.md)**: Formal definitions, derivations, and interactive 3D visualizations.
- **[Wavefunction Generator](../scripts/hydrogen_wavefunctions.py)**: A SymPy-based Python script to generate radial wavefunctions for any shell.
