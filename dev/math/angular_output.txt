Angular Wavefunctions (Spherical Harmonics - Real Forms)
Convention: phi=polar, theta=azimuthal
========================================================

--- s-orbitals (l=0) ---
m=0: 1/(2*sqrt(pi))
LaTeX: \rho = \left| \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{\pi}} \right|

--- p-orbitals (l=1) ---
m=-1: -sqrt(3)*sin(atan2(0, sin(phi)**2)/2)*cos(theta)*Abs(sin(phi))/(2*sqrt(pi))
LaTeX: \rho = \left| - \frac{\sqrt{3} \sin{\left(\frac{\operatorname{atan}_{2}{\left(0,\sin^{2}{\left(\phi \right)} \right)}}{2} \right)} \cos{\left(\theta \right)} \left|{\sin{\left(\phi \right)}}\right|}{2 \sqrt{\pi}} \right|
m=0: sqrt(3)*cos(phi)/(2*sqrt(pi))
LaTeX: \rho = \left| \frac{\sqrt{3} \cos{\left(\phi \right)}}{2 \sqrt{\pi}} \right|
m=1: -sqrt(3)*cos(theta)*cos(atan2(0, sin(phi)**2)/2)*Abs(sin(phi))/(2*sqrt(pi))
LaTeX: \rho = \left| - \frac{\sqrt{3} \cos{\left(\theta \right)} \cos{\left(\frac{\operatorname{atan}_{2}{\left(0,\sin^{2}{\left(\phi \right)} \right)}}{2} \right)} \left|{\sin{\left(\phi \right)}}\right|}{2 \sqrt{\pi}} \right|

--- d-orbitals (l=2) ---

m=0: sqrt(5)*(2 - 3*sin(phi)**2)/(4*sqrt(pi))

m=1: -sqrt(15)*cos(phi)*cos(theta)*cos(atan2(0, sin(phi)**2)/2)*Abs(sin(phi))/(2*sqrt(pi))
LaTeX: \rho = \left| - \frac{\sqrt{15} \cos{\left(\phi \right)} \cos{\left(\theta \right)} \cos{\left(\frac{\operatorname{atan}_{2}{\left(0,\sin^{2}{\left(\phi \right)} \right)}}{2} \right)} \left|{\sin{\left(\phi \right)}}\right|}{2 \sqrt{\pi}} \right|
m=2: sqrt(15)*sin(phi)**2*cos(2*theta)/(4*sqrt(pi))
LaTeX: \rho = \left| \frac{\sqrt{15} \sin^{2}{\left(\phi \right)} \cos{\left(2 \theta \right)}}{4 \sqrt{\pi}} \right|
